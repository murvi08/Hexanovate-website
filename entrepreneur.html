The Only Job in your Control...

This 6.9 minutes read, may change the path of your life!!

Do you wish to work for a company and use your brain to develop the company?

Is it possible for you to work in a cubicle from 9am to 6pm?

Is getting placed in a multinational company the only purpose of your life?

If answer to all the above questions is,
NO
Then, you are the perfect person to read this blog and expand your vision beyond jobs.
Here, I introduce a common term, a bit difficult to execute but definitely achievable.
" ENTREPRENEURSHIP "

Enterpreneurship, The real meaning

Being the most discussed topic among the youngsters around the globe, Entrepreneurship means studying the needs of the society, changing the need into a value added entity, garnishing it with innovation and creativity to transform it into something that people would be ready to pay for.

Entrepreneurship is identifying individual problems in a society with a different vision and probing solutions to the problem via software, marketing, business solutions and many more. It is a change that helps individuals to live with ease.

The increasing needs and demands of a society is the real cause which leads to making of more entrepreneurs.

One who possesses the vision to identify the problem, willingness to solve the problem and initiate the change for the future also called the entrepreneurial process is an ENTREPRENEUR.

Entrepreneurship is the need of the current world

Entrepreneurship is the need of today's society and in the coming days it will continue to get more and more importance. It does not simply imply being able to start and manage your own business.

It helps our country’s economy in many ways. A large share of the country's GDP comes from businesses. It would help create larger opportunities for employment, and enhance the standard of living of the people. It also helps in a faster community development.

Entrepreneurship may shape youngsters to think out of the box and research the market, where much of the population is suffering from the problem.

Greatest examples of Entrepreneurs who desired to bring the change:

  • Dhirubhai Ambani - Reliance
  • Narayan Murthy - Infosys
  • Ratan Tata - Tata company

What matters for us now is, How can we support the upliftment of these entrepreneurs?
How can we motivate the crowd towards this revolutionary movement?
How can we plan promotional sessions to imbibe entrepreneurial skills among youngsters?

Who and How can they help?

  • Governments and universities should take efforts to help the students understand the relationship between entrepreneurship and our economic development which would in turn create a huge economic and social impact for the country.
  • Multinational companies should conduct national level Hackathons. So that more students and graduates will get a chance to explore in their respective domain.
  • As a graduate, you should support your friend or colleagues to get connections and sources of contacts, so that one will get the right track to move forward in the right way.

If the above things happen, someone who really aspires to be an entrepreneur, can get a chance to live his dreams.
And then the bottom line is, more and more youngbies would get a chance to taste the sweet apple pie!!!

One Important question, that forces every individual to be an entrepreneur is,

Are Entrepreneurs billionaires?

A perception that entrepreneurs are billionaires, drives the major attraction for all to opt for Business and Entrepreneurship.

Is it false? No, it isn’t.

Not all entrepreneurs have become billionaires, but yes the ones who have achieved it, have not made it in a single night.

What made them different?

They created value for themselves and their products. They have stretched their abilities, their superskills, their knowledge to an extent where it has started earning profits in return. They created products for the betterment of their customer, and not for themselves.

Is there any difference between the life of an entrepreneur and a common individual?

How would an entrepreneur’s world be different from a corporate one?

Corporate life is comfortable and easy-going. In a corporate life, you work for fixed hours a day for which you would earn a fixed benefit. You work for someone, to earn them profit, and then somehow manage steady earnings for yourself. Corporate life has a steady and a linear growth path. Surely, you would be promoted and increase your earnings.

But wait, if someday you feel like trying out something different in your work, would you be able to do it?
Not all corporations allow you to work according to your own will.

But Yes, Entrepreneurship does.

What do you earn? Just becoming rich?
If you aim at just becoming rich, wait, you should think before having a step ahead. For setting up a business, you need a value. A value that people would be ready to pay for.
Create value for your product and for yourself. When your client realizes your value, you earn money. It is the only reason why only ideas can’t be an instinct to start a business. It needs vision and creativity, to bring it into life.

Can you see your image in the mirror as a future entrepreneur?

Reality lies in the fact that:

It is not always easy to plot down the needs of the society, manage the existing market and make your own firm stand in this vast world, being prepared for hurdles in the future and then apart from everything, plan profits for yourself.
It eventually needs a detailed observation, a planned and a hopeful mind, and some leadership qualities. Therefore, entrepreneurship is a sweet apple pie with a base of hopeful personality, icing of passion and garnished with knowledge.

There has been an immense discussion whether these entrepreneurs are made or born!

Someone who has a family business would simply learn from it and cultivate himself as an entrepreneur.

It is rightly quoted, Entrepreneurship is a LEGACY.

On the other hand, someone who is a newbie to this world, but has all the apple cake ingredients can successfully qualify himself as an entrepreneur.

Real Benefits of Entrepreneurship:-

What else you would earn, apart from money?

Job security?

Oh, you are the job creator now. You would employ people.

Can you work from home, or sit in the garden to work?

YES, you can work anytime and anywhere. You can have specific priority for your work.
Here you are your own boss. You wish to work just for 3 hours a day, you can (of course after your business is at its peak)!!!

What’s new in entrepreneurship?

Basically, entrepreneurship lessons are not taught in colleges, universities and business schools. These chapters of life get disclosed when we experience new things in life.

Entrepreneurship gives you an enthralling life. Each new day you have new plans. New risks to deal with. New people to meet. New techniques to learn. Even though it requires high passion and perseverance, it may be exciting for someone who wants to become his “own boss”.

Who should proceed in entrepreneurship? Who can be an entrepreneur? For whom is this Entrepreneurship?

For someone who wants to stretch his abilities and superskills. For someone who wants to climb up mountains of new risks each new day. For someone who wants to learn new stuff, each new day. For someone who wants to create employment opportunities and one who doesn’t like an easy going life.

It takes guts, to do something that the crowd would not aspire to do.

What makes you unique, is something that you do, is unique. Do you want to stand out of the crowd?

Young entrepreneurs of the world:-

  • Mark Zuckerberg - CEO of Facebook (age - 36 years)
  • Ritesh Agrawal - CEO of OYO (age - 26 years)
  • Farrhad Acidwalla - CEO of Rockstah Media (age - 26 years)

If you also have a zeal to create your own path and cater the needs of the society in a different way, begin your entrepreneurial journey right now.
There may be hurdles in the path but remember one thing, path is more important than the destination and in this journey you will come out as a personality leading the society.

I promise, It won’t be difficult but it will require at least 5x hard work as of now.

Are you ready, the future entrepreneurs?
