Content marketing “The Professor” of Digital marketing



Can you relate something after reading the title?


YES, you are right. If you have watched the most trending web series “Money Heist” you can relate it.


For others, let me give you a glimpse of it.


There is a character named ‘The Professor’ who was the mastermind of the heist. He was the sole controller of the heist. His wonderful tactics made the police work according to his plans.

Without him, the heist wouldn't have been successfully taken place and his group might have ended up being caught or dead.


In the same way, content writing and content marketing can grow your business by making your customer do what you exactly want them to do. That’s the reason why I introduced content marketing as the professor of digital marketing.

 

Importance of this strategy is still a mystery to many people and you might be one of them.

I bet by the end of this 7 minutes read, the mystery will be resolved.


Content marketing: The Mastermind 


If you didn’t hear about Money heist, the first question in your mind will be who is the Professor?

In the same way in regards to digital marketing, the first question is what is content marketing?


Content marketing is a strategy to attract, educate and aware your audience through the content you publish or post by providing them value, solutions to their problems and motivating them to action. Your quality content develops trust in your audience, urges them to take actions and ultimately make them your customers.


Content marketing isn’t a day or two stuff, it's a long and perennial process that needs consistency and quality content that generates a fear of missing out.

 

Content marketing is a vast domain and you can express your thoughts through a number of content forms viz blogs, videos, infographics, e-books and many more. Study says that videos are the most used content form. Infographics are new in the content marketing world because they are easy to understand.

Here are a few popular content forms with their percentage use.




Content marketing: The Main Contributor of Digital Marketing


The marketing practice over digital platforms like search engines, social media platforms, digital media etc is known as digital marketing. Digital marketing is a vast and diverse field whose core resides in content marketing.


The major digital marketing channels are,

  • Content marketing
  • SEO
  • Email marketing
  • Social media marketing
  • Advertisement
  • Mobile marketing


Digital marketing is an ecosystem and every channel of it is interlinked. This simply means that whatever strategy you will be working on but at the particular time you will be in need of other strategies to grow your business.


As I stated in my introduction that content marketing is the mastermind behind digital marketing and hence it has a role in every strategy.


What happens if I don’t use content marketing?

Let’s see all the strategies one by one to examine the importance of content marketing.


  • Starting from Search Engine Optimization (SEO) which helps in ranking your website on the search engine. One of the major metrics behind SEO is quality content and without quality content, it is impossible to rank your website on Google because it keeps user experience as its top priority.


  • Email marketing which is the best inbound strategy for direct sales and personalised conversations with customers need content too. 

We all receive hundreds of emails daily but how many of them do we open?

Every second 3.4 million emails are sent but only those emails get the attention that have high affinity content which can provoke users to take action. 


  • The new face of interaction with your friends and community is Social Media such as Instagram, Linkedin, Twitter and Facebook. Currently, there are billions of people on different social media platforms and many of them being the active users who use these platforms on a regular basis.. This happens because of the engaging content which is posted daily on social media platforms.


  • Many marketers think that they will invest some money and run an advertisement campaign. Even the ads cannot stand alone without content marketing. It's so hard to catch people’s attention so the ads also need quality content that can attract customers and urge them to take action very quickly.


  • Mobile marketing is used when we have contact information of our potential customers.

To your surprise every second more than 74k WhatsApp messages and 5k SMS are sent. It’s very hard to grab the attention of your target audience, when such a humongous amount of messages are being circulated on mobile. Here your excellent copywriting and content writing skills make you the winner.



Content Marketing: The Traffic Generator 


During the creation of a brand and while launching a product, content marketing is a useful strategy that brings customers to the businesses, companies and startups and prevents excessive use of money.


Now, we will see how content marketing works, 


Content marketing works as a Traffic Generator.

Content marketing converts readers obtained from organic search or social media into customers for a particular product.


It is important to understand that content marketing does not actually ‘create’ customers. Instead, content marketing provides readers with content which is written by proficient writers that attracts and engages them towards the product, services or company.


The conversion of audience to customers can be categorized in 3 broad steps-

  1. They find your content.
  2. They engage with your content.
  3. They become your customers.

This process appears simple but from the first step where we start with 100% audience to the last step we are able to have around only 3 - 5 % of them as our customers.


But the reality is, content marketing doesn't convert readers into customers so easily. As a generator requires more fuel to run. Same here, to increase conversion of readers to customers, it requires more valuable and engaging content without the break in consistency. 


Content Marketing : The Ultimate Benefits



There are many significant uses of content marketing. Let’s list out some of them:


  • Content marketing has the ability to increase your Brand awareness. By reading your quality content people might not become your customer but may share your content with others which will eventually increase your brand awareness.


  • Content marketing  ranks your website organically for a specific keyword. Organic ranking highlights your content at top positions of search engine result pages, which will attract an organic audience to your website. 


  • Content marketing  generates sales without investing money. You just need to invest time in making a Content marketing  strategy which can convert readers to customers.


  • Conversion rates are nearly 6 times higher for Content marketing  adopters than non-adopter. 


  • Content marketing  influences readers to be a customer because quality content gives them a lot of value and information.


New strategies will come and go but content marketing  is a long lasting strategy in digital marketing which will evolve continuously as time passes.

Stay tuned with your audience and create content in which they are interested. 

Content marketing  can change you and your audience’s vision and mindset regarding something. As we say a good content writer is eventually a good influencer. 


“Content marketing  is a bigger mastermind than ‘The Professor’ of Money Heist in the Digital marketing world.”


Now, you know it very well and I hope the mystery is solved.


It’s time to switch to content marketing after knowing its huge importance. Stop wasting your efforts on traditional and unplanned digital marketing. Analyze your audience and make a strategy for content marketing and you can see the results in the coming time.


70% of marketers are actively investing in content marketing and 72% of them say that it has increased their audience engagement. 


When are you going to test the power of this core principle of digital marketing i.e. content marketing?
