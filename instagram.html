Sorry Social Media, But Instagram is Fire!


Instagram, Oh Wow!

Scrolling amazing feed, sharing memes, stories, IGTVs and unlimited fun.

We all love instagram but did you even think of the following questions


  1. Why is Instagram so addictive?
  2. Is Instagram only for entertainment?
  3. Is there anything for my skills or business on Instagram?


None of us I guess. 

Let's move along and find out the answer to these simple yet important questions.

I am in college and like every college group, our dream trip was also a Goa trip. It isn’t simple, we all can relate. There is always one reluctant friend in every group and hence mine wasn’t unique. We also planned a Goa trip but encountered the same reluctance from a friend and no efforts to convince him were fruitful. 

Thanks to Instagram. I simply asked him to search #goatrip on Instagram and he was amused by posts of various Goa trips. “Our Goa trip post must also come on this hashtag”

We love pictures. So one of the reasons is that, “A picture speaks thousand words”. We have pictures on google too, so obviously there’s something more in Instagram.

1/7 th of World’s Population is on Instagram


Source - statista 2020

The user stats of Instagram shows the love of the younger generation for it. 1 billion of the world's population is on Instagram out of which 70% is below 35 years of age. Instagram is loved equally by both the genders. 

Money matters and hence we have 25 million business accounts on Instagram and Instagram is a source of income too.Today Instagram marketing is a must to have strategy for every business. 

Cristiano Ronaldo’s Income from Instagram is 47.8 million. 

Want to earn money from Instagram? We will find out this too in the coming sections.


Let’s go the start of Instant camera + Telegram = Instagram 

Back in 2010, Facebook was the most popular social media platform. There was something on Facebook, guess what?

Facebook didn’t focus much on image sharing. Kevin Systrom, the founder of instagram discovered that there’s a demand for photo sharing, as a medium of communication and hence developed instagram. 

We all might be knowing about an instant camera which gives you the image right after clicking it. Suppose we telegram this image depicting your fun and enjoyment to your friends. This is the job of your favourite application Instagram.

The idea was so unique that it gained huge popularity and Instagram’s growth and popularity got wings and is still increasing and has become one of the most loved and popular social media platforms.


Only Pictures aren’t the Reason for Instagram’s Popularity. More in the box!

What you love on Instagram?

Image posts?

Video posts?

IGTV?

Stories?

Live?

Sharing posts in DMs?

Instagram is a complete package. It has a lot of interesting features and hence gets one of the highest user engagement. Let’s explore all of its features.


Image post

You can post images on Instagram (maximum 10) and beautify them with the 40 + amazing filter and manual editing tools too. You can add up to 2200 character long captions and can use up to 30 hashtags.

A lovely image post by Selena Gomez

Video post

We all know video brings in great engagement. Instagram allows you to upload a maximum 60 second long video to your Instagram feed. You can post personal, educational, behind the scenes, motivational etc videos to your feed.

Here’s a crash simulation video by Tesla on Instagram

IGTVs

Want to try long videos?

Try the Instagram TV where you can upload longer videos and can put in various details. IGTV gets very much user engagement and moreover is shared a lot on Instagram.

Dwayne Johnson creates amazing IGTV videos

Stories

The heart of Instagram is its stories. It provides such a great level of engagement that people love to watch various stories. This is the reason they are placed at the top of the screen.

Instagram provides a number of features in stories like-

  • Text
  • Music
  • Boomerang
  • Superzoom
  • Layout
  • Various poll option
  • Ask a question 

It is one the most prominent reasons behind love for Instagram and users love to engage through stories.

Live

Let’s not only share photos and videos, get in touch with your friends or followers in real time through the live feature. 

Want to discuss some agenda, share live experience, and converse with the followers, Instagram live allows you to do all of these interactive communications.


Direct message

Instagram also allows you to chat with your friends as well as share content with your friends or followers.

Sharing memes, we all do that and love it too on Instagram.

It is very common that when we see a good post we share it to our followers or friends. 


Entertainment is not Enough, Instagram has a Bagful of Opportunities.

How many of you want to earn money or grow your business or startup?

You might be thinking that you will be having a marketing strategy for it. To your surprise there are 25 million business accounts on Instagram.

Instagram marketing is the promotion of our business or individual brand on Instagram. With 1 billion audience worldwide comprising mostly the young generation. Instagram has become the prominent part of digital marketing strategy of any prominent person, startup, business or even a person having some skills to showcase.

Lets look at some of the famous instagram accounts of some of the brands, celebrities and influencers.

  1. Apple (Popular phone brand)


  2. Gucci (Fashion brand)


  3. Katy Perry (Celebrity)


  4. Garyvee (Instagram influencer)


What we learn from the above examples is why to hide your skills or business?

Grab the opportunity you are having on Instagram and promote it.

People are getting customers, audience, income from Instagram.

Instagram business account gives you various features like analytics, ad campaign etc that will help you do the above mentioned jobs


You have skills, Instagram will give you a platform

If you have a talent, go for Instagram

If you have a startup, go for Instagram

If you have anything X that will add value to the society, go for Instagram

Social media have stepped further and have now emerged as a medium to grow audiences, reach people, target your niche audience etc. Instagram is one of the leading platforms in it.

Nowadays from a small coffee shop to a multinational company like GAP, from photographers like Steve McCurry to singers like Shakira, we find all skills and talents on Instagram.

Now it is your turn to uplift your skills, gain an audience and be successful.


Conclusion 

Now I think you are in a position to answer my initial three questions.

You know why it is addictive.

What is more important is that your perception for Instagram would have been changed and you will definitely find Instagram as a platform to gain success, audience, popularity and much many beyond.
