It’s high time to market your passion on Instagram



Digital India, a mission to make India digitally strong and aims to bring all businesses to the digital platforms. 

According to wikipedia, India stands second in the list of number of internet users with its 51.71 % of audience using the internet.


When will India become a true Digital India?


This is possible when all the businesses, no matter what their industry, target audience, niche or turnover is, make efforts to bring themselves on the online platforms. Digital platforms help businesses to target a larger audience, efficiently manage business operations and thus making it a more profitable business. 


The second most populated country where more than half of the population is using the Internet, how can any business think of missing the opportunity to set up themselves on a digital platform? 


Growing your business digitally using ‘Digital Marketing’ techniques is way more simpler. Social media marketing is a subdomain of digital marketing. It has an unique point of attraction for business owners. It provides high engagement of potential audiences and offers marketing at a comparatively lower cost.


There is one social media platform that has something unique to grow your business. A platform which has 1 billion user base worldwide and is mostly loved by youth.


Yes, you got it right, I am talking about Instagram, a social media platform that gave a new perspective to photo sharing and also coming up as the most efficient marketing technique.


Let’s gaze out at some impeccable stats about Instagram so that you understand why you need instagram to grow your business.

  • 25 million business accounts
  • Second largest audience base in India with 88 million users
  • Great window shopping experience
  • High engagement with average daily use of 53 minutes


Let's start with my personal experience.


My friend Abhishek who is a fitness trainer was trying to help clients by giving them fitness services in the gym. He was able to devote a maximum of 8 hours a day. He used to train every client for 2 hours per day and had a total of  4 clients.

He remained unsatisfied with his goal of helping more people and hence  he also had a low income.

I suggested that he should start giving online services. Now the problem here was which platform to choose? He experimented with some platforms and came to a conclusion that he will start marketing his services on instagram.

Now he is having more than 150+ clients worldwide and is working from home by giving them personalised diet plans and proper exercise videos in turn earning more revenue.


Are you still confused about the value that Instagram provides to your business?


Let's have a look how you should use Instagram to grow your business without spending more and reaching out to a unique audience.

  


4 benefits why to start your business on Instagram

 

  1. Offers Scalability and More Outreach


    2. Offline scalability has certain constraints to reach a limited audience but this isn’t the case with Instagram. Here you have an opportunity to reach millions of people through your content and daily updates. 


    The first thing you have to do is prepare a marketing and advertising strategy for Instagram.  After that you simply have to interact consistently with your audience and engage them with posting creative photos and videos of your products. Doing this will gain trust of your audience. This will help you to scale and grow your business faster by introducing more customers towards you.

       

  2. Less Expenses means High Profits


    3. It's true that every business is successful exactly to an extent when it tries to bring more profits than expenses as time passes. This can be achieved with the help of Instagram.


    Let’s see what exactly it means.

    In traditional marketing, for example you have to pay around ₹ 25000 for a newspaper advertisement while on Instagram you can run advertisements with even 100 times less cost of ₹ 250. Doing online business you are cutting out your huge costs of advertisements like banner advertisements, pamphlets and other expensive but less effective advertising mediums. 


  3. More Freedom and Flexible Work Hours


    4. How much time do you invest in strategic planning of your business growth?

    Most of you will say, we spend a huge amount of our time in running our business and managing day to day activities.

    If I say this problem can be resolved to an extent by switching to online platforms like Instagram.


    There is no doubt that scaling to online platforms gives you more freedom. You are not on a fixed working schedule which means you have got extra freedom. Value this independence and work harder on business growth because true entrepreneurs respect freedom and spend their time with families which most 9 to 5 job people miss out.


    It is better to stop business, if you don't have a future business plan.


    If we are stuck in only running the business and managing it, I am pretty sure that your business growth will stagnate. 


  4. World wide Market


Get closer to your world wide potential customers by setting up your business account on Instagram.

If you target just 0.01% of the audience in your niche, you will set up a crores of business per year.


Now you have a bigger network to approach which every businessman craves for. Your job is to just provide excellent services and showcase attractive products to engage users and lastly make them customers.


I am sure at this point of time you might be planning the onset of your Instagram marketing strategy. 


Hold On!!


Before your pack your seat belts and start the journey of growth of your business on Instagram, 


Let’s deep dive into some examples of popular personal business brands that are getting remarkable results from Instagram.


Travel Bloggers


Do you love to travel with your family, friends and mates?

While travelling, have you ever asked any guide for the correct way or proper destination?


You might be thinking, who are travel bloggers, how are they connected with instagram and how they really earn from instagram?


Let’s discover journey of travel bloggers on instagram,


  • They use their travel blogs to get freelance work or get paid for writing about the destinations from travel companies.
  • When you travel to some destination it is sure that you will click some amazing pictures and these pictures can make you earn money when you sell those to travel agencies.
  • If you are able to build a handful audience on Instagram then travel agencies contact you and ask you to travel and advertise your travelling scenes videos and pictures on your insta handle. 
  • You can also collaborate with lifestyle industries as they need an impactful and inspiring face.




You may know this famous personality Gaurav Taneja. He is a travel blogger and also a youtuber. In the above picture you can see he has collaborated with Ayada Maldives which is a beach resort. You can check that, he has tagged Ayada Maldives resort in his post.


Advertising specialist


If you think you have good imagination or you can make up your humour to create fun stuff then you should try your hand in this field. Let’s see how these skills bring magic to us.


  • Advertising products of other brands on your Instagram profile and help reach them out to more audiences by making attractive post content and using appropriate targeted hashtags.
  • You can create content such as blogs and articles to publicize small and large brands in the market.  
  • As time passes, you can build your instagram page for advertising other brands. After you gain more followers and have a decent social proof then, you can collaborate with brands to advertise and create posts for their brands. 
  • Nowadays meme advertising is trending because you can grow a brand with pushing humor content in your feed.





Madovermarketing is one of the best pages on Instagram for making creative advertisements for brands. In the above picture you can see how creatively phases of the moon are related to the stages of eating burgers. 


Food Bloggers


“If your mood is off and food makes you happy”. If food is happiness then it turns out to be a big niche.


People who love to eat and cook can stand out in this field. To attract epicures, you have to post mouth watering, yummy, sizzling and seducing food plates on your feed.


Check out the points, what you can do to monetize your passion for food.


  • You can showcase all the menus, recipes and ambience of restaurants to advertise their brand to the audience and eventually bring more customers to them. You can earn by taking commission from restaurants.
  • Making videos on description of hotels and their special dishes can help brands to grow their business faster and also help you to create your own personal brand.
  • You can collaborate and deal with restaurants for a year or less and promote their brands on digital platforms.
  • You can make a food blogging website and by marketing it on instagram you can bring traffic to that website.


These all the above strategies will let you earn a lot of money. The only skills you have to learn is marketing, photography and writing.


 


Here blogger is marketing the beer company (Bira91beer) and brewery (2downbeerco and yavasura). This is a perfect click for food bloggers to market their client.


Fitness Coach


You are a fitness freak and you have devoted some time of your day to fitness and health?  Now it's time to convert this passion into money. 


Online fitness coaches earn more on Instagram than their offline businesses, Shocked!! What to do?


  • You can guide your clients to lose weight, build muscles and also suggest them regarding intake of proper food required for their body.
  • Make a full fledged workout and diet course for your clients and sell them to worldwide audiences. 
  • After gaining a huge follower base on Instagram, you can collaborate with some fitness brands to publicize their products to your community. 




Miten Kakaiya is a fitness coach who lives in Mumbai and provides services all over the world. We can see that he is having apparel partners (Sweet Dreams), nutrition partner (ALMO) and big brand Fitternity as sponsors. He is also selling his 8 week transformation course in the above post.


I think now you can believe that your business can be marketed on Instagram and we can also earn more bucks as compared to offline marketing practices. If you want to build a personal brand, just bring your passion or talent on Instagram and engross the audience worldwide.


What are you waiting for? 


You just have to take a small step of setting up your instagram account and provide your audience with content that can encourage them to be your permanent customers.
