I know the value of content writing, do you?



I wasn’t a good content writer from the beginning and to your surprise I was among those who hesitate the most. In school or even in the initial days of college, whenever I was asked to write any article, report or essay, it was a dilemma for me and it used to take a lot of time. 


I realized that what is the use of learnings I acquired from my childhood if I cannot express it. I’ll tell you in the further journey of this blog what I experienced about content writing and its values.


Imagine the situation of your life, when you cannot express your opinions, give direction to yourself and share your experiences with the rest of the world. 


One which radiates faster to everyone and helps transform lives is Content. 


If you feel that you want to transform lives. And if you are ready to express your vision and experiences through writing then this next few minutes read is worthy for you.


In this blog, I will tell you the value of content writing that will modify your life and influence you as well as others to succeed in life.

 

Since schooling, we have been encouraged to write articles, reports, essays, etc. but then also when it comes to content writing, many of us hesitate to write. 


We don’t have enough confidence to write quality content. Have we ever found that what is the reason for this lack of confidence? 


Content, the very common yet unexplained term

In my realization of the value of content, the first thing that encountered my mind was what actually content is? I scrutinized my surroundings and I tried to find it in my daily life. Then, I came to this conclusion.


Is there any specific definition of content? 

NO


Content is very diverse and it cannot be bound in a simple definition. It ranges from written text to videos, infographics to podcasts and much more.


Content is anything which we see around us, may it be in any format. It can be texts, instagram posts, spectacular images, audios, and even trending videos.


If I am writing this blog, am I writing some “content”?

If I am watching videos on social media, am I watching some “content”?

If I am listening to songs, am I listening to some “content”?

If I am publishing a research paper, am I publishing some “content”?


Yes, these all are the forms of content. “Content is the presentation of any information that enhances your knowledge”. From nursery rhymes to solving a lot of mathematics problems, we have grown up learning content. We enhance our knowledge by reading books, watching youtube videos, joining online courses and exploring search engines and much more than this. We have got this information in the form of articles, courses, blogs etc.


Our learnings, opinions, views, thoughts are content and writing is one of the mediums to express these thoughts.


It is your mindset that makes content writing difficult

You all might have got an idea of content.    


It's so easy to visualize the content. When it comes to content writing, many of us will not be able to do it. 


Even when I started content writing, I was also going through the same phase. I used to think that my ideas and thoughts aren’t valuable enough and I need to practice more. I was inefficient in retrieving the right words to express my ideas. I found that task very tedious. 


So, what was the actual root cause behind my fear of content writing?


We have enough knowledge to express our opinions, views and thoughts over particular topics but what we lack is confidence to write any piece of information.


Practicing and maintaining consistency is the main aspect of expertising any job. 


Content writing is not difficult, and nothing in this world is difficult. It is just based on our self perception. Perception is based on our opinions. Opinions are based on our own thoughts. These thoughts come from our mind. So if you change your mindset then that makes things easy to proceed and execute.


Then, I realized that it was the result of my mindset which was subconsciously telling that writing was difficult.


Content writing is like a path to success in many ways. Anyone can walk on the path created by some successful people. Anyone can follow their footsteps. It takes little effort to go on that path but it takes time to make up your own path. Content writing is just like that. It's easy to copy and grasp what others write, but it takes time to give words to your own thoughts. 


The time when you make up your ideas in a flow then you are ready to write content.


Have you ever read a novel by Chetan Bhagat such as 2 States, The girl in the room 105, Half girlfriend and so on.


How do you feel while reading it? 

What arouses a feeling of love, passion and romance in you?


Just!! Arrangement of words


So what matters the most is, your arrangement of words while you write. Your words should be self explanatory for the reader. They should have the feeling of fulfilment. Your words should answer and satisfy the reader’s problem. 


For that, you need to give value to your own ideas and thoughts. There is nothing precious and valuable more than your thoughts. This will become the tool to make you stand unique in your content writing.


Writing should be your habit to express your thoughts and empower others. Even if you write once a week then, it will drastically improve your writing ability.


Why is it so important to write content?

The importance and benefits of content are the major driving force for me in realizing its value. Did you bother why I am emphasizing so much on content writing despite there being humongous blogs on the internet? 

We have been encouraged to write content but no one tells us why?


There are a number of benefits and some being very necessary for us as a student or job seeker. Let’s discuss them.


When you get a motive to do something productive for yourself then you do it very passionately. Here are the reasons that motivated me towards content writing and it will surely convince you to start it.



  • Content writing enhances your communication skills


“When you write, you are forced to think and be more articulate”

                                                                                                            --Jeff Bezos


It persuades you to think. There are a lot of subjects, ideas and topics that many of us typically do not explore. When you write, it increases your thinking ability. This means that when you decide to write, you’ll be forced to think on that particular topic.


Writing consistently develops the power of speech. Writing makes you more articulate. You’ll develop the ability to speak fluently and coherently.

Content writing improves your vocabulary


“Choice of words” is the prime concern in every means. In writing also “choice of words” matters a lot. You have to put efforts in your word choice for writing. It can take time to find the words that better capture the situation in a piece of writing. You have to be patient with your thoughts or looking up for synonyms. 


  • Content writing enhances your writing skills and knowledge base 


Along with communication skills, content writing enhances writing skills as well. Writing consistently speeds up the rate at which your brain processes and deals with information. 

There may be situations when you want to write on topics that are totally             unknown to you, it will require establishing a knowledge base. The improved writing ability and knowledge base will make you smarter as well as expert in writing and communication.


  •  Content writing boosts confidence


There will be many situations when you are required to put your efforts in writing a research paper, article in a very short time. The words will need to flow from the mind to your finger to the page. It takes a different level of complexity to think in various situations. In this kind of situation, we lose our confidence. Writing makes you more confident in your own ideas and opinions.  


  • Content writing helps you improve your market value 


We have our future goals as to be an entrepreneur or get a job. When you go for a job, content plays a vital role. Knowledge is not only enough to get a job. Primary thing that a company considers for placement is expertise in vocabulary.


Content writing is important because it enhances your communication skills in a number of ways. Some companies demand for great writing expertise and they spend a lot of money on content writers.


  • Content can help you earn money


Content marketing, the centre of the new digital era. What am I talking about? Let me clear this. The old days where the sales person came knocking the door, we read ads in the newspaper and hoardings on the roadside, companies calling individuals to sell their products, does all this work now? 


Rightly guessed. Even I won’t like to buy products with the help of these traditional marketing techniques. Because traditional techniques lack the value of the product. Before buying a product I always think about the value that the product adds to my life. 


The value of the product stands as a content there. The modern day sales are done with the help of valuable information about the product. I hope you might have got what role content plays in selling a product. So we say that it can earn you money. You can use your content writing to earn a huge amount of money as a copywriter.

Yet a long story of how to earn money using content, we will discuss that later in our further blogs.


Conclusion

Here I come to the end of my story which was all about realization and the value of writing. Testimonial for my story is this blog which I wrote with full exuberance, devotion and positive energy.


Once you realize the value of content writing and how it is going to change your life, it will expertise you in many fields and build your overall personality.


If you practise writing content to convey your opinions and suggestions to the world through writing, your ideas will not be bound by your inner self but will come out and guide everyone.


It is rightly said that, 

Creating value is not enough, you also need to capture some of the value you create. 


I captured some of the qualities and incentivized myself to start content writing. I have articulated my opinions and feelings via. writing to guide society.

If you also want to enrich your skills then start writing by today itself. 

If you are still thinking that you will not be able to write content then make it a challenge to yourself. And trust me once you’ll start writing, there will be a drastic change in yourself.


Good Luck and all the best for a new journey!!
