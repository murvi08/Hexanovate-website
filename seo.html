Origin of the term SEO


Imagine, you are a Librarian of the biggest university in the world. You have a huge storage of books and students come to you to get information for their queries. You search their answers in your library in related books. It is a complex job to give the students what solutions they need. 


Search engines also work similarly, they search for the user's query in their huge database or index and come up with the user’s solution. Same here,  search engines have these kinds of libraries and search engines are the librarian but the difference is, search engines follow an algorithm to find a user's solution in its database and provide the user with the most relevant content in a very short time span. 


To make your content relevant and to match that content according to the search engine algorithm’s requirement. Businesses and website owners have to follow the process called SEO.


When we talk about SEO, the first word which comes to our mind is GOOGLE. After all, Google has very high market shares upto 91%, making it the most popular search engine. But the tactics we will be discussing are applicable to other search engines like Bing and Yahoo as well.


SEO: This is what professionals do!



SEO stands for search engine optimization. SEO in simple words is the free way to gain quantity and quality traffic to your website and grow your business faster.


The world of search engine optimization is very complex and dynamic, but you have to learn and expertise all SEO basics to sustain in this competition. Even a handful of SEO knowledge can make a big difference in your technical skills and also in your business. 


Free SEO education is also widely available on the web. So what are you waiting for? 


If you want to see your website on the first rank among thousand others then you have come to the right place. This short guide will explain the basics and major aspects of SEO. Combine this knowledge with some practice and surely you will become a SEO expert.


What SEO pays off for you?


We know that higher the ranking, higher CTR (click through rate) will be and that is how we will attract our customers. 


Have you ever visited the second page of Google SERP?


If you are not even in top 10 sites, then the user is not going to have a look at your site. Users don't click on the sites which are on the second page. They try to find the solutions over their problems in the first SERP only. So if you want to reach out to more audience and increase your business then you have to set your position among the top ones and key to do this is SEO. 


 

The main motive of the search engine optimization is to get more and more traffic from various sources and to rank your web pages for certain words. SEO plays an important and crucial role in ranking business websites as well as other interactive sites and shows optimised web pages in front of the user's problem.


What you can do with SEO?

  • Rank freely
  • Create online presence
  • Drive online traffic and generate more revenue
  • Leverage social sharing 
  • Improve user experience

 

User experience is the most important factor to rank on SERP. Ranking of webpages is based on users. For Ex. More users click on the second link than the first link then Google’s algorithm will rank the second link over the first one.  

User’s trust in search engines and having presence of your website in the top positions in SERP for a particular keyword, increases the website's trust. 


Behind the scene of search engine


In a simple way, search engines are the machines which answer the user's queries by performing certain algorithms. When a user performs any search, the search engine finds the relevant information in it’s huge, organized and maintained data in its databases so it will show you the most relevant content according to preferences.

Let’s dive deeper into the working of SEO:-


We can divide SEO's working into 3 main factors

  • Crawling 
  • Indexing
  • Ranking


Crawling? Do you really need it? This will help you decide!  


Search engines have a huge fleet of bots they call them crawlers and spiders. which search for the keywords in the content regardless of the format it may be image, video, text. 


These bots also go through all links in the webpage. It's like a tree. You can imagine the pages structure as a tree linked in between each other. These bots start from the root of the tree and search the whole tree until they reach the end of every branch. Now they have another database of discovered URLs, which will be used when any user wants any information.


In simple words Crawling means following a path. In the SEO world, crawling means following the links present on your site and “crawling” around your website. When bots come to your website’s web pages, they follow other linked pages which are also present on your website.

Factors that affect crawling?

  • Domain name
  • URL
  • Title tag, Meta description
  • External linking/Backlinks
  • Internal linking/Crosslinks
  • Duplicate content (Plagiarised)

INDEXING? It's Easy If You Do It Smart  


What happens once a search engine has finished crawling a page? Let’s take a look at the indexing process that search engines use to store information about web pages, enabling them to quickly return relevant, high quality results to users as per demand.


Indexing generally refers to getting your web pages (or other various forms of content from PDFs to videos) indexed in GOOGLE or in any other search engines. A search engine will crawl your website in search of content to add to its index.


Indexing is a way to optimize the performance of a database by minimizing the number of accesses required when a query is processed. 


It is a technique which is used to quickly locate and access the data in a database. Search engines have a database of all discovered URLs.

An index is another name for the database used by a search engine. Indexes contain the information of all the websites that Google (or any other search engine) is able to find. If a website is not in a search engine’s index, users will not be able to find it.


The Ranking mystery revealed! 


Ranking simply means making the web pages more likely to get ranked on a search engine. In SEO, ranking refers to the position of your content search engine results pages(SERP). #1 ranking means when user searches some particular term your page is the first result(apart from paid results/Ads)


Rankings in SEO refers to a website's position in the search engine results page(SERP). Various ranking factors influence whether a website appears higher on the SERP based on the content relevance to the search term there are many factors which are responsible for ranking your website. Here are some main factors:-


Most important SEO ranking factors :

  • Optimized content
  • Secure and accessible website
  • Mobile  friendliness
  • User experience
  • Page speed
  • Information on the page(Unique)
  • Linking
  • Clear and unique Domain name
  • URL (User Friendly)


If you  will try to optimize these factors then surely your site will be in top most search results.

It will not be an easy task unless you are not paying money for it. Users generally skip the ads and choose the first link for their query so you have to increase your ranking organically.
